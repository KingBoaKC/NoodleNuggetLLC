About Us

The Story Behind the Noodle

A small company with a big belief: that good thinking, plain language, and genuine care can solve almost anything.

Mission

Creative Solutions to Real Problems

NoodleNugget exists to be the resource that individuals and small businesses wish they'd had sooner.

We're part IT support, part creative consultancy, and all about making complex things approachable. Whether it's a computer that won't cooperate, a network in need of some love, or a business challenge that needs fresh eyes. We show up ready to noodle through it.

Based in Kansas City, we serve clients on-site across the metro and remotely wherever you are.

What We Stand For

How We Approach Every Engagement

Four principles that guide how we think and how we work.

Plain Language, Always

We don't hide behind jargon. If you leave a conversation more confused than when you started, we haven't done our job.

Root Cause Over Quick Fix

Band-aids wear off. We dig into why something is broken so it stays fixed, and you understand what happened.

No Tech Shame

There are no dumb questions here. Everyone starts somewhere, and we're patient, encouraging, and judgment-free.

Creative Thinking First

We bring curiosity and creativity to technical work. The best solution isn't always the obvious one. Sometimes it takes a noodle.

Security-Minded by Default

We consider your security and privacy in everything we do, not as an add-on, but as a baseline expectation.

Small Business Heart

We are a small business. We understand the constraints, the stakes, and what it means to need something to just work.

The Name

Why "NoodleNugget"?

Because that's exactly what we do.

Noodle: to think, to mull, to work through something carefully. When you've got a tough problem, you noodle on it. That's us, every day.

Nugget: the good stuff. The insight. The "aha" at the end of working through a problem. That's what we're always after for our clients.

Put them together and you've got a company that thinks hard and delivers real value. Plus the logo is pretty great.
